## Why Sponsor?
- **For Solo Supporters**: Back open-source tools like Osmedeus for cyber adventures. Perks include early updates or nods in docs. Bonus: Tax perks in some areas—smart way to boost impact.
- **For Organizations**: Feature your logo on project pages for targeted visibility. Sync with tech experiments for mutual wins. Just note: Projects evolve, so stay tuned.

It's like tipping a communal innovation pot—everyone wins from the brew.


## How to Sponsor

- **One-Off Boost**: Drop a quick donation via Paypal or Kofi for simple fuel injection.
- **Steady Support**: Monthly via GitHub Sponsors or Patreon. Levels: `$5` for sneak peeks, `$20` for extra flair.
- **Non-Cash Help**: Spread the word on X, Telegram, Discord, Github or pitch in code amplifies without spending.
